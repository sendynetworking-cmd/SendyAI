Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Sendy AI ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Sendy AI Chrome extension and website (sendynetworking.io).

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in via Google OAuth, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.

Resume Data: If you upload your resume during onboarding, it is processed to personalize your email drafts. Resume data is stored securely and only used for email generation.

LinkedIn Data: When you use the extension on a LinkedIn profile, we extract publicly visible profile information (name, headline, experience) to personalize outreach messages. We do not store LinkedIn data on our servers beyond the duration of the request.

Usage Data: We track the number of credits used and your subscription tier to manage your account.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized networking emails on your behalf.
  • To manage your account, credits, and subscription status.
  • To improve the quality of our AI-generated content.
  • To respond to support requests and inquiries.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Anthropic (Claude): Profile and resume excerpts are sent to our AI provider solely for email generation.
  • Supabase: User account data and usage records are stored securely in our database.
  • ExtensionPay: Subscription and payment processing is handled by ExtensionPay / Stripe.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), OAuth 2.0 authentication, and secure cloud infrastructure to protect your data.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at sendynetworking@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at sendynetworking@gmail.com or via our contact page.
