FAQ's

You asked, we answered.

Still got questions? Feel free to reach out to our incredible support team, 7 days a week.

How are Sendy AI's emails personalized?
Sendy AI uses your synced profile and resume to craft highly personalized outreach emails. It analyzes the LinkedIn profile of your target contact and combines both data points to generate a relevant, professional message tailored to each individual.
Is Sendy AI free to use?
Yes! Sendy AI offers a free tier with 10 credits per month. You can generate personalized emails, look up email addresses, and experience the full AI outreach workflow at no cost. Upgrade to Pro for 250 credits and access to the latest LLM models.
What should I use Sendy AI for?
Sendy AI is designed for networking, job outreach, and professional relationship building. Use it to reach out to recruiters, hiring managers, alumni, mentors, or anyone on LinkedIn you'd like to connect with via a personalized email.
Does it work on mobile devices?
Sendy AI is a Chrome extension and works best on desktop browsers. The generated emails can be opened and sent from any device, including mobile, via Gmail.
